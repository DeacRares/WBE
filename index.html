Weapon Balls ELO Rankings

        Weapon balls elo rankings (WBER), is a NPO that is ranks every weaponball in Earclack's famous "Weapon Balls" series,
    using an elo engine to determine who is the best weapon ball. Those rankings only count 1v1s happening during the regular
    season the season end tournament, and doesnt count free for all, team events and block breaker.
    *note : In best of 3 matches I count each individula game so if a weaponball won 2-1 I count it as 2 wins and 1 loss
